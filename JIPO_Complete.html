Help

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does trademark registration take? +

Trademark examination typically takes 3–6 months once the application is submitted. The total process from filing to registration can take 8–12 months if no objections are raised.

Do I need a lawyer to file a patent? +

You can file a patent yourself, but we recommend engaging a registered patent attorney for complex inventions. JIPO's e-filing system guides you through each step, and our staff are available to answer general process questions.

How do I load funds to my account wallet? +

Log in to your Client Portal and click "Top Up Wallet" on the Wallet tab. You can add funds by credit/debit card, bank transfer, or NCB QuickPay. Funds are credited instantly and can be used to pay filing and registration fees.

Can I register IP internationally through JIPO? +

Yes. Jamaica is a member of the Paris Convention and the PCT Network, allowing Jamaican filers to seek protection in over 180 countries. JIPO can advise you on the Madrid System for international trademark registration and the Patent Cooperation Treaty for patents.

Established 1999 · Kingston, Jamaica

Built on Expertise.
Driven by Purpose.

The Jamaica Intellectual Property Office is the government authority responsible for administering and promoting intellectual property rights in Jamaica. We combine deep legal expertise, technical knowledge, and a commitment to public service to protect innovation across every sector of the Jamaican economy.

Our Mission

Administer IP rights with integrity, efficiency, and expertise.

To provide a world-class intellectual property system that supports economic growth, encourages innovation, and ensures Jamaican creators and businesses can protect and benefit from their ideas — at home and internationally.

Our Vision

A Jamaica where every idea is valued and protected.

We envision a nation where intellectual property is understood, respected, and actively used as a tool for economic development — where every artist, inventor, and entrepreneur knows their rights and can exercise them with confidence.

Who We Are

JIPO is a government statutory body established under the Jamaica Intellectual Property Office Act. We operate under the Ministry of Industry, Investment and Commerce and are Jamaica's official authority for the registration and administration of trademarks, patents, industrial designs, copyright, and geographical indications.

Our team includes qualified intellectual property attorneys, patent examiners, trademark examiners, copyright officers, and outreach specialists — all working together to ensure Jamaica's IP system is accessible, modern, and aligned with international standards.
